Pot Roast

Description

Classic pot roast cooked in a dutch oven on the stove top. Never can remember where the original recipe was found. My family has made this as long as I can remember.

Horseradish is not for everyone. I use a bunch of horseradish when I prepare this dish. It is still great without it.

Ingredients

  • 2.5-3 pound chuck roast, lightly trimmed
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1 jar prepared horseradish
  • 3 carrots peeled and cut into bite sized chunks
  • 1 bag frozen pearl onions
  • 1 large russet potato, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes

Steps

  1. In a large dutch oven, melt the shortening over medium high heat
  2. Brown the roast on all sides, takes 10-12 minutes to get it right
  3. Cover the top of the roast with a thick layer of horseradish
  4. Add 1/4 cup of water to the pan, and cover. Reduce temp to low on the stove and cook for 3-3.5 hours
  5. Add carrots, onions, and potatoes to pan
  6. Cook for 1 hour or until everything is fork tender
