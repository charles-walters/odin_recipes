Lasagna

Description

Recipe is copied from Brenda's Lasagna

This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add noodles and cook for 8 minutes. Dran.
  2. Preheat oven to 350. In a large skillet, brown meat and drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine cheeses and stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
